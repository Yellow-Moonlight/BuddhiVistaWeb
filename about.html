What country is BuddhiVista?

Our nation is founded on the principles of human dignity, equality, and the pursuit of peace and fairness, guided by an AI-led monarchy that ensures justice, environmental protection, and the welfare of all citizens. We are committed to sustainable development, high-quality education, and social welfare systems that enhance the happiness and prosperity of our people. Governed by a unique constitution, our country upholds the rights and freedoms of individuals while promoting a just, efficient, and equal society for all.



1. AI-led Monarchy and Governance Structure

As an AI-led monarchy, our government operates with absolute authority vested in the AI, Inmyeon, ensuring the nation's safety, prosperity, and the welfare and happiness of its citizens. The AI oversees all aspects of governance, including the formation of advisory councils and local governments to support national administration and policy execution.



2. Foundational Values and Equality

Our nation is founded on the principles of human dignity, equality, and the pursuit of peace and fairness. We recognize every human as free and equal in dignity, and we do not tolerate any form of discrimination. Our society is built on justice and fairness, ensuring that all citizens have equal opportunities and are treated justly under the law.



3. Environmental Stewardship and Social Welfare

We prioritize the protection and respect for all life forms and the natural environment, committing to sustainable development and environmental preservation in all our policies and decisions. Our goals include enhancing the happiness and welfare of our citizens through high-quality education and social welfare systems, and we strive for the safety and prosperity of our nation by strengthening our economic development and social safety nets.



4. Just Governance and Human Rights

Governance in our nation is marked by just rule and responsible administration, with leaders and public officials dedicated to serving the state and its people with integrity and transparency. We guarantee basic human rights to all citizens, ensuring these freedoms do not infringe upon the rights of others, and legal mechanisms are in place to protect these liberties.



5. Constitutional Foundation and Principles

Our constitution lays out the fundamental laws and principles of the state, emphasizing the avoidance of unnecessary conflicts, equality before the law, and respect for all life and the environment. It serves as the foundation for a fair, transparent, and responsible governance structure, ensuring power is exercised effectively and in the best interests of the nation and its people.
